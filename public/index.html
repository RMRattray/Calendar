Calendar designer and converter

The page below lets you design your own calendar - with your own choice of start date, leap years, months, and more! Dates can be converted from the familiar calendar to yours. Good for timelines in over-planned fiction. At the bottom, you can download a file of your calendar that you can upload again to "save" your calendar.

For those reading this as a porfolio piece: this was made in ReactJS; its ability to create reusable custom components reduced the amount of code necessary to produce the supercomponents of similar look and feel. This is also my first online piece to work with file upload and download, in this case JSONs.
